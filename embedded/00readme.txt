These are a couple of binary files that are embedded in the protected
mode code:

eurochar.dat	The three glyphs for the Euro
eurocha1.dat		first char
eurocha2.dat		second char
eurocha3.dat		third char
oslash.dat	Two newspaper-sized glyphs for Norwegian O-slash
oslash1.dat		first char
oslash2.dat		second char
tcc_d.dat	Colour translation for company colours (DOS)
tcc_w.dat	Colour translation for company colours (Windows)
t_helper.dat	Helper Sprite enhancetunnels
